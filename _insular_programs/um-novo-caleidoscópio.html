More than 250 videos participated in the Open Call. From the difficult choice among so many proposals, the twelve selected demonstrate the diversity of the Portuguese artistic panorama. The projects translate into images, words, sounds and bodies the issues that cut across our societies. Let us listen and dwell on the vibrations of these promises, these fragile plants that flourish in between the ruins.
