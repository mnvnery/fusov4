In this fictional scenario, the Palestinians inhabit their own state, represented by a towering skyscraper known as the Nation Estate. where the entire Palestinian population lives. Each floor represents a city. The journeys between different cities, once regulated by checkpoints, now occur in elevators with cyber controls, where a fingerprint or iris scan is sufficient.
