Recitations of Popular Songs are heard amidst various other sounds. This recording became the basis for a video which is, at the same time, comparative (then and now) and expositive, by depicting the metamorphosis of the space. The Association played a fundamental role in the lives of all the workers of Barrosa, and also for their daughters and sons. imagem: /assets/img/3-—-still-—-associação-livre-dos-trabalhadores-da-barrosa.jpg credito_imagem: ©Zé Castanheiro layout: evento ---
