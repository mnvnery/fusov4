Using geospatial data—ranging from river basins to land use and economic indices—the piece exposes how mapping, a seemingly neutral practice, serves capitalist ventures and territorial control. The video juxtaposes vibrant, algorithmically generated maps with a critical reflection on the digitisation of land as a form of modern colonialism. Accompanied by stereo soundscape, the installation questions whether contemporary datafication is a rebranded, insidious continuation of historical exploitation.
